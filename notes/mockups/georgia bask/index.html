Search & image posts

July 28, 2015

Your blog now comes with a built-in search feature. You can now include a form in your blog’s template which makes a GET request to /search with a query named q. Try it out on my blog, to the top right of this page.

When Blot receives a request to/searchwith a valid query then it will return a page of results. Search is currently implemented using REDS, a simple library made by the creator of Express. It’s incredibly easy to add and remove strings from the index, but currently impossible to weight keys.

Also, the posts below this one are image posts. All I had to do to publish them was move the image files into Blot’s folder. Blot created a caption from their file name!

They were both righteous before God, walking blamelessly in all the commandments and ordinances of the Lord. But they had no child, because Elizabeth was barren, and they both were well advanced in years. Now it happened, while he executed the priest’s office before God in the order of his division, according to the custom of the priest’s office, his lot was to enter into the temple of the Lord and burn incense.
