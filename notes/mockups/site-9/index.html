Use your favorite text-editor to write your blog.

Blot is a blogging platform which turns a folder into a website. There’s no interface. Just files and folders.

Blot creates a folder in your Dropbox and publishes files you put inside automatically. Images, text files, Markdown, Word Documents™ become blog posts.
A site costs $30 per year. Hosting is included and there are no ads. All of Blot's templates are available to you.

Blot’s seven templates are designed to be customized. If you’re comfortable with HTML and CSS, you can also create your own template from scratch.
