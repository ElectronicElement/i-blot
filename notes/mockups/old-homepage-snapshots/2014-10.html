Blot is a blogging platform.
It adds a folder to your Dropbox
and publishes files that you put inside.

Use your favorite text editor to write. Publishing is as easy as dragging and dropping a file.

Blot works with images, text, HTML and Markdown files.

Blot comes with four themes or you can create your own.

You have complete control over your blog on Blot.

Will Blot ever have advertising?

No, never.

Why does Blot cost money?

I have to cover the cost of running Blot somehow.

Can Blot access all the files in my Dropbox?

No, Blot only has access to the folder containing your blog posts.

Will my blog use up my Dropbox bandwidth?

No, Blot fetches a copy of each blog post and hosts it on Blot's servers.
