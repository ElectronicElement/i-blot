Another blogging platform?
Blot is a blogging platform designed for your favourite text editor. It adds a folder to your Dropbox and publishes files you put inside automatically.

Images, text files, Markdown, Word Documents™ (and more) become blog posts. There’s no interface. Just your files.

Members pay $30 per site, per year. This keeps Blot fast and reliable. Hosting is included and there are no ads.
