Blot is a personal blogging platform that turns a folder into a blog. It adds a folder to your Dropbox and publishes files you put inside automatically.

This means you can use your favourite text editor to blog. Images, text files, Markdown, Word Documents™ become blog posts. There’s no interface. Just your files.

Members pay $30 per site, per year. This keeps Blot fast and reliable. Hosting is included and there are no ads.
