Blot turns a folder into a blog
Sign up · Help & Support · Log in







Blot is a blogging platform. It turns a Dropbox folder into a blog. Any files you put inside are published automatically.

Images, text files, Markdown, bookmarks, Word Documents™ (and more) become blog posts. There’s no interface. Just files.

Why does Blot exist? So that you can use your favorite text editor to blog.

Members pay $30 per site, per year. Hosting is included and there are no ads.
