Blot is a blogging platform. It creates a folder inside your Dropbox and publishes files you put inside. This means you can use your favorite text editor to write your blog.

Publishing with Blot

These files become blog posts when you put them somewhere in your site’s folder:

  • Text .txt, .md
  • Images .png, .jpeg, .gif
  • Word documents .docx
  • Bookmarks .webloc
  • HTML .html

There’s no interface. Just files and folders.

Templates

Blot comes with seven templates. They are designed to be easy to customize. If you know a little HTML and CSS you can create your own template from scratch.

A site costs $20 per year. Hosting is included and there are no ads. Here’s a handful of the sites hosted by Blot:
