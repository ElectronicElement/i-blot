Blot is a blogging platform. It turns a folder in your Dropbox into a blog. Files you put inside are published automatically. Images, text files, Markdown, bookmarks, Word Documents™ (and more) become blog posts. There’s no interface. Just files and folders.

Why does Blot exist? So that you can use your favorite app to write your blog. Vim or Emacs or Microsoft Word™? You decide.

Blot’s seven templates are designed to be customized. If you’re comfortable with HTML and CSS, you can also create your own template from scratch.
