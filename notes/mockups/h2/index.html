Blot is a publishing platform which turns a folder into a website.

Why does Blot exist?

So that you can use your favorite app to write and publish.

How does it work?

Blot adds a folder to your Dropbox account. Just drag-and-drop files into Blot’s folder to publish them. Images, text files, Markdown and more become posts automatically. There’s no publishing interface. Just files and folders.

How much does it cost?

Members pay $30 a year to keep Blot fast and reliable. This covers the cost of hosting your site, fixing bugs and answering your questions.

Can I choose how my blog looks?

You can use one of Blot’s themes or design your own. Hosting is included and there are no ads.

How long has Blot been running?

I started working on Blot in the summer of 2013. Blot launched a year later and has grown sustainably since.

How long do you plan to run Blot?

Decades, at least.
