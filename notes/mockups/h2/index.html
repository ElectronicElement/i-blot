Welcome to Blot, a low‐to‐no‐effort publishing platform which turns a folder into a website.

Why does Blot exist?

Blot exists so that you can use your favorite tools to create and publish. More about Blot.

How does it work?

Blot adds a folder to your Dropbox account. Just drag‐and‐drop files into Blot’s folder to publish them. Images, text files, Markdown and more become posts automatically. There’s no publishing interface. Just files and folders.

How much does it cost?

Members pay $30 a year to keep Blot fast and reliable. This covers the cost of hosting your site, fixing bugs and answering your questions in a timely manner.

Can I move my current site to Blot?

Yes, Blot comes with tools to import posts from Wordpress, Tumblr, Squarespace and other blogging platforms. Blot's themes share many similarities with other platforms.

Can I use my own design?

Yes, you can customize every line of HTML and CSS used on your blog. Read more about this on the developer page.

How long has Blot been running?

Blot launched in the summer of 2014 and has grown sustainably since.

How long do you plan to run Blot?

Decades, at least.

Can I host Blot on my own server?

Yes, Blot is open‐source and dedicated to the public domain.
