Blot turns a folder into a website. You can use your favorite tools with Blot to create and publish.


How does it work?

Blot adds a folder to your Dropbox. Files you put inside are published automatically. Blot converts text, images, links, Markdown and more.

Why does Blot exist?

Since Blot's interface is just files and folders, you can use your favorite app to write. More about Blot.

How much does it cost?

Members pay $30 a year to keep Blot fast and reliable. This covers the cost of hosting your website, fixing bugs and answering your questions.

Can I move my website to Blot?

Yes, Blot comes with tools to import posts and pages from Wordpress, Tumblr, Squarespace and other platforms. Blot's themes share many similarities with other platforms.

How long has Blot been running?

I began work on Blot in the summer of 2013. Blot launched a year later and has grown sustainably since. I plan to keep Blot running for decades, at least.

Can I host Blot on my own server?

Yes, Blot's entire source code is dedicated to the public domain.
