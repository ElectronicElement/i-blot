## Drafts

```folder
Drafts
  Anecdote.txt
  Anecdote.txt.html
Pages
  About.txt
  Contact.txt
  Resources.txt
Posts
Archive.zip
```

Files inside a folder called 'Drafts' are not published to your site. Instead, Blot creates a preview file which you can open in your web browser.

## Pages

Files inside a folder called 'Pages' are added to your [site's menu]({{menu-and-profile}}) instead of the list of blog posts.
