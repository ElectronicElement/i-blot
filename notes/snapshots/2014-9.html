Blot is a blogging platform.

Blot creates a folder in your Dropbox and converts any text files you put inside to blog posts.

Play the demo

Your favorite text editor. Blot converts files in your Dropbox folder into blog posts. This means you can write with your favorite text editor. Watch the demo.

Looks beautiful. Blot comes with two carefully designed themes and works hard behind the scenes to typeset your entries. See a blog using Blot.

Easy formatting using Markdown. Blot supports entries in plain text, Markdown or HTML. You can even embed your own scripts.

Fast and reliable. Blot hosts blogs with thousands of posts with ease. Your entries are cached on Blot's servers. Downtime is rare and brief.

Features

  • Hosting for your blog
  • Two beautiful themes
  • Custom domain support
  • Support for custom themes
  • Posts in markdown or HTML
  • Embed scripts in blog posts!
  • RSS feed & sitemap for each blog
  • Automatic code highlighting
  • Disqus comment support
  • Math typesetting support


Can Blot access all the files in my Dropbox?

No, Blot only has access to the folder containing your blog posts.

Will my blog use up my Dropbox bandwidth?

No, Blot fetches a copy of each blog post and hosts it on Blot's servers.
