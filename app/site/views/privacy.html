Do not store sensitive information on Blot. The contents of your site's folder are hosted on Blot's servers and can be accessed by visitors to your site.

If you spot a security breach, please report it to me. If there is a security breach, I promise to disclose it on Twitter. You can delete your account and all the data associated with it at any point.

Blot does not share the data it stores with third parties and never will unless compelled by law. Blot stores the following information about you:
  • Your email address
  • Your password
  • A list of the sites you run on Blot
  • The contents of the sites you run on Blot
  • A token to initiate subscription payments through Stripe which you can revoke at any point by deleting your account
  • A token to read and write to Blot's folder in your Dropbox which you can revoke at any point on Dropbox

The only usage data Blot collects is the number of requests per day and the time it takes to handle each request. Blot may temporarily monitor URIs with high response times during periods of exceptional load.
