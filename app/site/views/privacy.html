Privacy policy

Do not store sensitive information on Blot. The contents of your site's folder are hosted on Blot's servers and can be accessed by visitors to your site.

You can delete your account and all the data associated with it at any point.

Blot does not share its data with third parties and never will unless compelled by law. Blot stores the following information about you:

  • Your email address
  • Your password, which is hashed using bcrypt
  • A list of the sites you run on Blot
  • A token to initiate subscription payments through Stripe which you can revoke at any point by deleting your account
  • A token to read and write to Blot's folder in your Dropbox which you can revoke at any point on Dropbox's settings page

The only usage data Blot collects is the number of requests per day and the time it takes to handle each request. Blot reserves the right to temporarily monitor URIs with high response times during periods of exceptional load.

If you spot a security breach, please report it to me. If there is a security breach, I promise to disclose it on Blot's status page and on Twitter.
