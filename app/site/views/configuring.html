Using your own domain

Every blog comes with a subdomain that you are free to use. You can also configure your blog on your own domain if you prefer. Read the full guide.

Importing blog posts from elsewhere

I’ve written scripts to generate a folder of files from Tumblr, Wordpress, Jekyll and Squarespace. If you contact me, I will run them on your site and send you the result.

Typography

Blot includes a number of features designed to improve the typography of your blog. You can add syntax highlighting to code, render mathematical statements in Tex into HTML and check your text for common typographical improvements.
