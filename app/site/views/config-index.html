Configuring your blog

All of your blog’s configuration is done on the dashboard. It’s where go to select a template for your blog, adjust some of Blot’s settings and set up your blog on your own domain.

Templates

Blot comes with seven templates. They’re responsive and work well on small and large screens. You can also develop your own template from scratch. Here is the selection of templates:

Using your own domain

Every blog comes with a subdomain that you are free to use. You can also configure your blog on your own domain if you prefer.

Guide to using your own domain

Importing blog posts from elsewhere

I’ve written scripts to generate a Blot-friendly folder of files from sites built on Tumblr, Wordpress, Jekyll and Squarespace. If you contact me, I will run them on your site and send you the result.

Typography

Blot includes a number of features designed to improve the typography of your blog. You can add syntax highlighting to code, render mathematical statements in Tex into HTML and check your text for common typographical improvements.
