Default permalinks

Each post is assigned a permalink, or permanent URL. You can edit the general format of these permalinks on the dashboard. You can use any of the post's properties and any of Blot's date tokens. Here are a few examples:

The permalink format... will produce URLs like...
{{slug}} example.com/title-of-the-post
blog/{{slug}} example.com/blog/title-of-the-post
{{YYYY}}/{{slug}} example.com/2017/title-of-the-post
{{YYYY}}/{{MM}}/{{D}}/{{slug}} example.com/2017/10/3/title-of-the-post
{{slug}}-{{MM}}-{{YYYY}} example.com/title-of-the-post-10-2017
