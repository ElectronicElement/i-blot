Blot is a blogging platform with no interface. It turns a folder into a blog. If you put files inside, Blot publishes them. Why does Blot exist? So that you can blog with your favorite text-editor. Because Blot is file and folder-based, you can use your favourite creative tools.

Blot automatically creates blog posts from
 Text and Markdown,
  Word Documents,
  Images,
  Bookmarks and  HTML. Just put the files inside your blog’s folder and Blot does the rest.
