Blot is a blogging platform with no interface. It creates a special folder in your Dropbox and automatically publishes files you put inside. The point of all this — the reason Blot exists — is so you can use your favorite tools to create whatever you publish.

Blot automatically creates blog posts from Text and Markdown, Word Documents, Images, Bookmarks and HTML. Drag‐and-drop the files into your folder and Blot does the rest.
