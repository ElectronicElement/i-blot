Blot is a blogging platform with no interface. It turns a folder into a blog. If you put files inside, Blot publishes them.

Why does Blot exist? So that you can blog with your favorite text-editor. Because Blot is file and folder-based, you can use your favourite creative tools.

How to use Blot

Blot automatically creates blog posts from Text and Markdown,  Word Documents,  Images,  Bookmarks and  HTML. Just put the files inside your blog’s folder and Blot does the rest.

Read the documentation

Templates

Blot comes with seven templates. They’re easy to customize, lightweight and designed to fit both small and large screens. You can also develop your own template from scratch.

Blot comes with a number of features to refine the typography of your blog. This includes true hanging punctuation and common typographic substitutions with Typeset. Blot also renders mathematics in \(\TeX\)and provides syntax highlighting for code.
