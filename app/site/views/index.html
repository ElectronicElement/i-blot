Blot is a blogging platform. It turns a Dropbox folder into a blog. Any files you put inside are published automatically.

Images, text files, Markdown, bookmarks, Word Documents™ (and more) become blog posts. There’s no interface. Just files. You can use your favorite text editor to blog.

Members pay $20 per site, per year. Hosting is included and there are no ads.
