Blot stores various data about you, your blog{{user.s}} and your blog posts. On this page you can download most of that data as a JSON file. This file will include:

  • Your account settings
  • Your blog settings
  • Your templates
  • Metadata for all of your blog posts

This file will not include:

  • The files inside your blog’s folder. If you want to backup your blog posts, please make a copy of your blog’s folder directly.
  • The token used by Blot to access its folder in your Dropbox.
  • Your payment information, since it is not stored on Blot’s servers. Stripe handles payments for Blot.
