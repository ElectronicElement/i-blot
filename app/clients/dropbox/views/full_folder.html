  • Share your blog’s folder with other people who use Dropbox
  • Move and rename your blog’s folder

If you store sensitive data in your Dropbox folder, please contemplate the consequences of a security breach. Consider using a throwaway Dropbox account, then sharing your blog’s folder with your main account and those of your collaborators.
