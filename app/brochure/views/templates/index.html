Templates

Blot comes with seven templates.

  • Console Designed for essays, code
  • Default Designed for short posts, photos, links
  • Grid Designed for photos, links
  • Magazine Designed for long essays
  • Photos Designed for photos
  • Portfolio Designed for photos
  • Rosa Designed for short posts, photos, links

These templates are designed to be customized. Their source code is released into the public domain and is available to you. You can also develop your own template from scratch. Blot gives you complete control over every line of HTML, CSS and JS delivered to your readers.
