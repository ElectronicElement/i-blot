Templates

Blot comes with seven templates. All of these templates are designed to be customized. Their full source code is available to you and dedicated to the public domain. You can adjust an existing template or develop your own template from scratch.

  • Console Designed for essays and code
  • Default Designed for short posts, photos and links
  • Grid Designed for photos and links
  • Magazine Designed for long essays
  • Photos Designed for photos
  • Portfolio Designed for photos
  • Rosa Designed for short posts, photos and links
