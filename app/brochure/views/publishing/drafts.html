Drafts

Files in a folder called ‘Drafts’ are not published. Instead, Blot creates a preview file which updates as you make changes to your draft.

Sharing drafts

You can share the preview file with others. Preview files work on other computers that are connected to the internet. You can also get a public URL to a draft on your blog's dashboard.
