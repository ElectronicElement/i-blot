Pages

Files inside a folder called ‘Pages’ become pages instead of blog posts. Links to each page appear on your blog’s menu.

Hidden pages

You can hide a page from your blog’s menu by overriding the ‘Menu’ metadata:

Menu: no

A link to this page will not appear on your blog's menu.

Create a landing page

You can change the permalink of any post or page. To create a landing page, change the page's URL:

Permalink: /

This page will replace your blog's homepage.

If you still want your readers to access the list of your blog posts, consider adding a link to /page/1 to your blog's menu.
