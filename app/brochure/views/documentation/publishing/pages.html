Pages

Files inside a folder called ‘Pages’ become pages instead of blog posts. Links to each page appear on your blog’s menu.

Hidden pages

You can hide a page from your blog’s menu by overriding the ‘Menu’ metadata:

Menu: no

A link to this page will not appear on your blog's menu.
